Short Bio

Dimitris Kontokostas is a Knowledge Engineer and Semantic Web researcher at University of Leipzig and the DBpedia Association. He is close to hold a Ph.D in Semantic Web from the University of Leipzig and is the technical director of DBpedia since 2014. His (research) interests focus around knowledge representation and knowledge management of large-scale knowledge graphs and data quality. Dimitris is a co-editor of SHACL, the upcoming W3C recommendation for data validation and the creator of RDFUnit, a popular tool and unit-testing framework for RDF validation.

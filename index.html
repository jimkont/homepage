Short Bio

Dimitris Kontokostas is a principal software engineer at the Medidata Knowledge Graph. Before Medidata, he was the lead software engineer of the Diffbot knowledge graph and the technical lead of the GeoPhy Data Platform before that. Dimitris has a PhD (Dr.-Ing.) from Leipzig University (AKSW/KILT Group) on "Large-scale knowledge extraction, publishing and quality assessment" where he graduated with magna cum laude.
